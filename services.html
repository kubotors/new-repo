Kubotor Cybersecurity Solutions- Services

Kubotor offers a wide range of cybersecurity services, designed to protect, assess and enhance the resilience of digital environments against modern threats. Our services focus on thorough assessments, advanced insights and practical solutions that address the unique needs of each organisation.

Our Services

ISMS (Information Security Management System)

We help you implement robust Information Security Management Systems (ISMS) to protect sensitive data and ensure compliance with ISO 27001 standards.

  • Risk Assessment: Identifying, evaluating, and mitigating security risks within your organization.
  • Compliance with ISO 27001: Ensuring that your organization complies with the internationally recognized standard for information security management.
  • Data Protection & Confidentiality: Protecting sensitive and confidential data to prevent unauthorized access and breaches.
  • Continuous Monitoring & Improvement: Regularly monitoring and improving security measures to stay ahead of evolving threats.

Security Testing

Our vulnerability assessments and penetration testing (VAPT) services identify security weaknesses in web applications, mobile apps, APIs, and infrastructure.

  • Vulnerability Assessment: Identifying vulnerabilities in your systems, applications, and infrastructure.
  • Penetration Testing (VAPT): Simulating real-world attacks to identify exploitable weaknesses and how to fix them.
  • API Security Testing: Ensuring your APIs are protected from external threats.
  • Mobile & Web Application Testing: Focusing on securing mobile and web applications from cyber-attacks and vulnerabilities.

Offensive Operations

We simulate phishing attacks, conduct ethical hacking, and perform offensive reconnaissance to identify vulnerabilities and strengthen your defenses.

  • Phishing Simulation: Simulating phishing attacks to raise awareness and test an organization's response to malicious emails.
  • Ethical Hacking & Illegal Access Testing: Conducting ethical hacking to identify vulnerabilities in your systems, simulating a real-world cyberattack.
  • Offensive Reconnaissance: Gathering intelligence on target systems to improve defensive security measures.
